RATTLESTAR
RICKLACTICA

For the NASA Space Apps International Challenge, me and my team had the problem statement of creating a idea of a habitable exoplanet. We explored this topic on fictional as well as realistic level while creating a model of our own planet Rattlestar Ricklactica on Blender which was inspired from the show Rick and Morty

Self Irrigating
Plant Pot

I build a pot attached with a water tank, a soil moisture sensor, actuators and Arduino-uno. The project was designed and sensor was calibrated in such a way that depending upon the moisture level of the soil, if the moisture was low, using a DC pump, the water would be transferred from the water tank to the soil through a pipe for timely and appropriate irrigation of the plant in the pot

A.M.R.I.T

A.M.R.I.T stands for Automated Medicine Retailing with Intelligent Technology. This is a currently ongoing project being built by me and my team for Smart India Hackathon. The project's aim is to have a QR code enabled vending machine which when scans a QR prescribed by a doctor engraved with a digital signature for verification purposes, will automatically dispense the prescribed medications list stored in the QR. This is done so as to reduce the waiting time at pharmacies and chemist shops for the convenience of patients.
